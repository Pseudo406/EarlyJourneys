Ready For School Room

  • Home
  • Our Rooms


At Early Journeys in Ready for school room we follow AISTEAR the Early Childhood Curriculum Framework and we even look after every individual child’s way of thinking and develop a special thinking based curriculum. Here we work on getting the children ready for school by explaining to them and working with them about what it is going to be like when they go to the school. We explain to the children about the acceptable and unacceptable behavior, we make sure that all our kids when they go to the school are familiar with the alphabet and phonetic sounds; we encourage learning of numbers 0-10 by listening & writing with counting. In the ready for school room we start of the day with ECCE kids where we follow the start with circle time, free play, art and craft activities, and many more things. All our activities and educational work is showcased to the children in a fun and play based way, to encourage the child’s involvement.

When a child is ready to go to school they have a lot of questions in their mind about nature, the environment, science, and many more general knowledge questions in depth.

We aim for the wellbeing of the children and let them explore with a broad thinking mind.
